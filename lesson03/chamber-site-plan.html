Overview

Purpose

Washougal resides on the Washington side of the Columbia River. Just 20 minutes outside of Portland resides a city built on locally owned and operated stores and restaurants. I wish to promote these small owned businesses and become a new weekend getaway for people all around. Furthermore, Washougal deserves to be recognized for the value of a fostered community. With an emphasis of community, the city prompts for shared economic and service development.

Scenarios

What businesses are holding local events? What kind of opportunities does Washougal have for upcoming businesses? What does the future of business look like in Washougal? Where can I contact members of the board? What is the weather climate like?
