Rule of Thirds

Nike Website

The rule of thirds is the process of breaking up content using a grid. The grid consist of 9 equal squares. Where the lines interesct is where the eye line falls. This is to be utilized for content to be easily accessible to the human eye. Also, it allows for an easier flow of content on a page versus being rigid.
